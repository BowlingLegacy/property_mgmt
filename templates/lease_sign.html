1. Premises and Purpose

Residents are granted the right to occupy a room at The Painted Lady Inn, a permanent housing option committed to sobriety, self-sufficiency, and community support. Residency is contingent upon compliance with the terms below.

2. Term of Residency

  • Start Date: {{ signed_document.lease_start_date }}
  • End Date: Month-to-month unless terminated earlier pursuant to Section 10
  • Renewal: Automatic month-to-month renewal unless otherwise agreed in writing

3. Monthly Rent and Payment Terms

  • Base Rent: ${{ signed_document.monthly_rent }} per month
  • Due Date: Rent is due on the 1st of each month
  • Collection Date: Rent is collected on the 3rd
  • Late Fee: $25.00 after the 5th pursuant to ORS 90.260
  • Returned Check Fee: $50.00 pursuant to ORS 90.302

4. Shared Utilities

Residents contribute to shared utilities including electric, gas, water, and garbage.

  • Current Utility Fee: ${{ signed_document.utility_fee }}
  • Utility adjustments may occur based on occupancy
  • 30-day written notice provided for changes

5. Periodic Rent Adjustments

  • Rent increases take effect January 1 annually
  • No increase exceeds Oregon limits under ORS 90.323
  • Residents may submit hardship requests for review

6. Resident Responsibilities

  • Maintain cleanliness in common areas
  • Avoid excessive trash, clutter, or food waste
  • Clean private rooms regularly
  • Respect fellow residents and house harmony
  • Participation in recovery programs is encouraged but not required

7. Manager Housing and Support

The Painted Lady Inn may provide on-site housing for a manager who supports daily operations, enforces house rules, and models recovery.

8. Property Improvements and Access Notice

  • Property is undergoing phased remodeling
  • 24-hour notice provided whenever possible under ORS 90.322
  • Residents agree to cooperate with reasonable access requests

9. House Rules and Charter Conditions

Serious violations may result in termination under ORS 90.392 or ORS 90.396.

  • Alcohol or illegal drug use
  • Being under the influence on the property
  • Disruption of house harmony or safety
  • Failure to pay rent or utilities
  • Repeated failure to maintain cleanliness
  • Refusal of reasonable testing

10. Termination of Residency

  • 24-Hour Notice pursuant to ORS 90.396
  • 10-Day Notice pursuant to ORS 90.392
  • Standard nonpayment notices apply
  • Resident may terminate with 30 days written notice

12. Sober-Living Environment and Resident Responsibilities

This residence operates as a sober-living community. All residents agree to maintain complete abstinence from alcohol, illegal drugs, and misuse of prescription medications.

Refusal to submit to reasonable testing is considered a violation of residency requirements.

Residents agree to maintain safety, cleanliness, and harmony in all shared living spaces.

17. Guests, Family, and Community

  • Guest policies exist to protect safety and harmony
  • Immediate family may visit during normal visiting hours
  • Non-family guests require manager approval
  • Guests must be introduced upon arrival
  • Residents are responsible for guest behavior
  • No guests after 10:00 PM without approval
  • First 90 days guest restrictions apply

Lead-Based Paint Disclosure

Housing built before 1978 may contain lead-based paint. Residents acknowledge receipt of lead awareness information and agree to report peeling or damaged paint immediately.

Asbestos Disclosure

Residents acknowledge receipt of asbestos awareness information and agree not to disturb suspected materials.
