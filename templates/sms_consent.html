SMS Opt-In Example

Bowling Legacy Resident Text Message Consent

This public page shows the SMS consent language used when a resident chooses to receive text messages. Residents are not required to opt in to rent from Bowling Legacy.

By providing your mobile phone number and selecting this checkbox, you agree to receive account-related SMS text messages from Bowling Legacy. Messages may include resident portal notifications, rent reminders, utility reminders, maintenance notices, building notices, and urgent property updates. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of renting from Bowling Legacy. Bowling Legacy will not sell, rent, or share your mobile information with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.
