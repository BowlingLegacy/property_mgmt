The Creed

Our guiding principles for life, leadership, and service

The Creed reflects the values that guide Bowling Legacy Housing — how we treat our residents, how we lead, and how we show up in the world.

  1. Always do the right thing, even when no one is looking.
  2. Protect and serve those unable to protect and serve themselves.
  3. Don't let hate take root in your heart; life is too short for negativity.
  4. Always be respectful to and of others.
  5. Forgive and move on; don't harbor ill will.
  6. Don't lie — you only deceive yourself.
  7. Be part of the solution, not the problem.
  8. Help your brother or sister whenever you can.
  9. Remember: the best leaders are the best followers first.
  10. Make everyone around you important, and you become important to them.
  11. Be thankful for each day — even the worst ones.
  12. Listen to understand, not to respond.
  13. Be the one who makes things happen.
  14. Never break a promise.
  15. Believe in a life without hate or anger; there is something positive in every situation.
