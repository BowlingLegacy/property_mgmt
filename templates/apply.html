Housing Application

Bowling Legacy Housing provides sober, structured, respectful housing. No drugs or alcohol may be brought onto the property or consumed while a resident.

We do not require attendance at recovery meetings, but we fully support residents who attend.

Our primary house rule is: Unconditional regard for others.

Questions about recovery, parole, or felony history help us understand housing barriers, support needs, and whether Bowling Legacy may be a good fit. A felony history does not automatically disqualify an applicant.
